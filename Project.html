About us

Adidas, founded in 1949 by Adolf Dassler, is a global leader in the sporting goods industry. With a legacy of innovation and performance, Adidas produces a wide range of athletic and lifestyle products, including footwear, apparel, and accessories. The brand is known for its iconic three stripes logo, commitment to sustainability, and collaborations with top athletes, designers, and cultural influencers. Adidas continuously strives to push the boundaries of sportswear technology and design, making a lasting impact on the world of sports and fashion.
